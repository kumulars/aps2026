FOR ATTENDEES Personalized Agenda: Attendees can create personalized schedules, receive reminders, and access event information on their mobile devices. Networking: Facilitates connections through attendee profiles, one-on-one messaging, video conferencing, and discussion forums. Engagement: Encourages participation through live polls, surveys, Q&A sessions, and gamification features. Information Access: Provides easy access to event details, speaker information, maps, and other logistical information. Mobile Check-in: Allows for quick and efficient check-in using QR codes and mobile devices. Sponsor and Exhibitor Interaction: Offers opportunities for attendees to connect with sponsors and exhibitors through dedicated pages and booths.
